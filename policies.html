Freshness & Quality Guarantee

Every order is graded to export standard and cooled within hours of harvest. We stand behind it completely.

If anything arrives that is not perfect — bruised, under-ripe, or simply not up to standard — tell us within 24 hours of delivery. We will replace it on your next order or refund it, whichever you prefer. No long forms, no debate. Quality is the whole promise, and we treat it that way.

Delivery

We deliver across New Cairo, Sheikh Zayed, October, Madinaty and Rehab.

  • Complimentary delivery on orders over EGP 600.
  • A flat EGP 45 delivery fee on orders below EGP 600.
  • Next-day slots: morning (9–12), midday (12–3), afternoon (3–6) and evening (6–9).
  • You'll receive an SMS update when your produce is on its way.

If you are not home, our driver will call. We keep produce cold in transit, so a short wait does it no harm.

Returns & Replacements

Because we sell fresh produce, we do not accept general returns for change of mind. What we do offer is simpler and more generous:

  • Quality issues: reported within 24 hours are replaced or refunded in full.
  • Wrong or missing items: we send the correct item, or refund it, right away.
  • Boxes: contents adapt to the season; if a substitution isn't to your taste, let us know and we'll make it right.

Payment

We accept credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), Apple Pay, mobile wallets, and cash on delivery.

Card payments are processed by a certified payment provider. We never see or store your full card number — saved cards are held securely with our provider, not on our servers. Prices are shown in Egyptian Pounds (EGP) and include applicable taxes.

Subscriptions

Any box can be set to a weekly or fortnightly schedule. You are always in control:

  • Pause, skip or change your delivery day at any time from your account.
  • Cancel whenever you like — there is no minimum commitment.
  • We'll remind you before each charge, so there are never surprises.

Privacy

We collect only what we need to deliver your order well: your name, contact details, address and order history. We use it to serve you, and to send the updates and seasonal notes you've asked for.

We do not sell your data. You can ask us to update or delete your information at any time by writing to hello@freshvalley.eg. For the full detail, see our Terms of Use.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, please ask. We'd rather explain than leave you guessing — get in touch.
