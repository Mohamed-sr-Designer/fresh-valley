# UX Copy & Voice

## Voice principles

Write like a thoughtful host, not a supermarket. English, **B1–B2** — simple, warm, human,
clear. Never corporate, never salesy, never aggressive.

- **Quiet, not loud.** No "SALE", "BEST PRICE", "BUY NOW!!". Confidence is shown, not shouted.
- **Generous, not pushy.** We *invite*; we don't pressure. "Make it a table, not a plate."
- **Concrete, not abstract.** "Picked at full colour and cooled within hours" beats "premium quality".
- **Short sentences.** One idea each. Plenty of full stops.
- **Hosting lens.** Tie produce to people and moments — guests, gatherings, the morning bowl.
- **Honest.** "If it isn't perfect, we replace it." Promises we can keep.

### Do / Don't
| Don't (grocery) | Do (Fresh Valley) |
|---|---|
| "Best deals on fresh fruit!" | "Premium produce for better hosting." |
| "Add to cart now & save" | "Add 1 kg · EGP 120" |
| "100% satisfaction guaranteed" | "If it isn't perfect, we replace it." |
| "Subscribe & never run out" | "Your produce, on a quiet schedule." |
| "Limited time offer" | "What is best, right now." |

## Key copy deck (as shipped)

**Hero:** Premium produce for *better hosting.* / Fruit and vegetables, graded to the
standard the world's best tables expect — chosen for the moments you share at home.

**Announcement bar:** Complimentary delivery on orders over EGP 600 — New Cairo, Sheikh
Zayed, October, Madinaty & Rehab.

**Hosting Culture H1:** Fruit and vegetables are no longer just groceries. They are part of
*hospitality* — part of the gathering, part of the moment.

**Behind the Quality:** Graded like an export house. / Nothing reaches your table by accident.

**Cart free-shipping nudge:** Add **EGP X** for complimentary delivery. *(progress, not pressure)*

**Empty cart:** Your selection is empty. Nothing here yet — let's fix that.

**Add-to-cart toast:** {Product} added · {variant} — View cart.

**Order confirmation:** Your table is on its way. / We've received your order and started
grading your produce.

**Quality guarantee (Policies):** If anything arrives that is not perfect … tell us within
24 hours. We will replace it or refund it. No long forms, no debate.

**Newsletter:** Eat with the season. Host a little better. / A quiet note each month … No noise.

## Microcopy conventions

- Currency: `EGP 1,250` (prefix, thousands separator). Helper: `FV.money()`.
- Weight variants: `250 g`, `500 g`, `1 kg`, `2 kg`. Piece items say "each"; herbs "per bunch".
- Buttons are verbs in title case: "Add to cart", "Place order", "Explore the box".
- Eyebrows are short, uppercase, often numbered on Home ("01 — Who is Fresh Valley").
- Badges: "Export-Grade", "Organic", "Seasonal". Never a discount badge.
- Cart is called **"Your Selection"** — a curated, considered word, not "Basket".
